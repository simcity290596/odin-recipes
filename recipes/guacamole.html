Guacamole

Ingredients

  1. 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  2. 1 lime, juiced
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  5. Â½ cup diced onion
  6. 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  7. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  8. 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Directions

Step 1

Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.

Step 2

Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
