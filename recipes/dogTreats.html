Discription

Baked dog treats filled with pumpkin and peanut butter.

Your furry friend is an important member of the family, and that means they deserve homemade treats too! Sure, you can go out and buy a box of dog treats, but these Peanut Butter and Pumpkin Dog Treats are made with love â€” and they're just as easy to make as making a batch of cookies.

Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Flour
  • Eggs
  • Canned Pumpkin
  • Peanut Butter
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon
  • Water

Steps

  1. Making homemade dog treats is just as easy as making cookies for you and your family. In fact, they're prepared in almost the same way.
  2. Simply combine all your ingredients in a bowl or stand mixer. Then transfer the mixture to a work surface and work it with your hands until a dough has formed.
  3. Roll out the dough and either cut it into strips or stamp it with your favorite cookie cutters.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F for 40 minutes, until crunchy. Make sure to wait until they are completely cool before giving it to your dog.
